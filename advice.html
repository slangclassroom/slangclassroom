What's your advice regarding slang?

Generally, you should incorporate both slang and more formal language into your lessons to teach students when to use which. For example, you could maybe compare formal and informal language, or stress the importance of media literacy.

The most important thing is to discuss slang, to make your classroom inclusive language-wise, and to have empathy for your students. Perhaps you could allow students to use slang amongst themselves, while still encouraging them to use more formal language in specific situations.

In the end, it's up to your own discretion how you incorporate slang into your curriculum, but we do advise you to be more on the lenient side.
