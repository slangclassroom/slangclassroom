    Slang

What are the advantages and disadvantages of slang?

Slang can have many positive effects, from making students feel more comfortable and increasing motivation, confidence and participation due to a more positive atmosphere in the classroom. Furthermore, students who are familiar with slang are generally more adept language-wise – although they might struggle with writing formally. Banning slang also might feel like a personal attack on one’s identity.

However, banning slang reinforces standard language, which is tied to greater success further in life. You wouldn’t want to be calling your job interviewer unc, for example, so teaching your students not to use that kind of language in certain situations might help them avoid that kind of thing in the future.
